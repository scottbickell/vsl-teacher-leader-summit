The Fundamentals of Literacy Instruction for Principals

When teachers understand the WHY behind the HOW to teach reading, they can improve their literacy instruction and increase student achievement.
LETRS® (Language Essentials for Teachers of Reading and Spelling) is a literacy professional development course of study proven to help educators deepen their understanding of literacy instruction and improve student outcomes. Through “hands-on” demonstrations, attendees will learn about LETRS' approach to the science of reading and how it provides teachers with the knowledge and tools to teach language and literacy skills to ALL students.

SESSION TWO:
Build the Foundation for Success in Kindergarten and Beyond

  • 1:00 p.m., Wednesday, June 26, 2019

Presenter: Helen Long

National Education Consultant at Voyager Sopris Learning

Establishing developmental readiness for foundational skills in preschool is essential for children’s success and achievement in school. We Can® Early Learning Curriculum, a proven preK and early learning solution prepares all children, ages 3–5, by boosting the rigor in preschool to prepare them for kindergarten and beyond. We Can cultivates young learners and provides a clear road map for early learning success, including easy-to-implement lesson plans and a range of engaging and fun learning strategies, resources, and tools that may be used immediately in the classroom.
